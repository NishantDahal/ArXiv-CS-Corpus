C++11 - określanie typów

This paper presents a review of some new futures introduced to C++ language by ISO/IEC 14882:2011 standard (known as C++11). It describes new language elements which allow to easier expressed of types of variables: auto and decltype keywords, new function declaration syntax, and tools which are included in type_traits header.
