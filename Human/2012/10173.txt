CODYRUN, outil de simulation et d'aide à la conception thermo-aéraulique de bâtiments

This article presents the CODYRUN software developped by University of La Réunion. It is a multizone thermal software, with detailled airflow and humidity transfer calculations. One of its specific aspects is that it constitutes a research tool, a design tool used by the lab and professionnals and also a teaching tool. After a presentation of the multiple model aspect, some details of the tree modules associated to physical phenomenons are given. Elements of validation are exposed in next paraghaph, and then a few details of the front end.
