Pre-proceedings of the 26th International Workshop on Functional and Logic Programming (WFLP 2018)

This volume constitutes the pre-proceedings of the 26th International Workshop on Functional and Logic Programming (WFLP 2018). It is formed of those papers selected by the program committee for presentation at the workshop. After discussion at the workshop, the program committee will select a number of papers to be invited for the second round of refereeing and selection for the formal proceedings.
