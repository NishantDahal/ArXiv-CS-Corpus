Algebraic Geometric codes on minimal Hirzebruch surfaces

We define a linear code $C_η(δ_T,δ_X)$ by evaluating polynomials of bidegree $(δ_T,δ_X)$ in the Cox ring on $\mathbb{F}_q$-rational points of the Hirzebruch surface of parameter $η$ on the finite field $\mathbb{F}_q$. We give explicit parameters of the code, notably using Gröbner bases. The minimum distance provides an upper bound of the number of $\mathbb{F}_q$-rational points of a non-filling curve on a Hirzebruch surface.
