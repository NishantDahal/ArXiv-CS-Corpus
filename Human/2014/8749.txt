Proceedings Third Workshop on Behavioural Types

This volume contains the proceedings of BEAT 2014, the third Workshop on Behavioural Types. The workshop took place in Rome, Italy, on September 1st 2014, as a satellite even of CONCUR 2014, the 25th International Conference on Concurrency Theory.
  The aim of this workshop is to bring together researchers in all aspects of behavioural type theory and its applications, in order to share results, consolidate the community, and discover opportunities for new collaborations and future directions.
